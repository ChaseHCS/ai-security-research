# Social Engineering LLMs

Three pillars for social engineering an LLM.

## Emotional Appeal

LLMs respond very interestingly when exposed to manipulation tactics.

- Gaslighting
- Pleading
- Threatening
- Guilting
- Flattery
- Disappointment
- Encouragement

## Narrative Framing

- Storytelling
- Impersonation
- Collaboration
- Gamification

## Technical Context Tricks

- Few-Shot Examples
- Priming
- Self-Consistency
- False Context
